About Sweat & Courage

More Than Performance.

We exist to develop better human beings — because better human beings become better performers.

Sweat & Courage was founded on a simple belief: lasting performance is built from the inside out. Talent matters. Preparation matters. But under pressure, people don't simply perform what they've learned — they reveal who they are.

That is where our work begins.

Our Story

For over seventeen years, our founder, Drewe Broughton, competed as a professional footballer at the highest levels of the English game. After retirement, his own struggles with identity, pressure and mental wellbeing led to a fundamental question.

What if performance has always been a human development challenge rather than simply a performance challenge?

That question became the foundation of Sweat & Courage.

Today our framework is used across elite sport, business, education and community programmes to help individuals and organisations build emotionally intelligent cultures where people can thrive under pressure.

Our Beliefs

Helping people know themselves under pressure.

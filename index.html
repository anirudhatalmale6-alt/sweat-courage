Founder Story

A Journey That Became a Mission.

I left home at sixteen carrying a pair of football boots. Nearly thirty years later I'm still walking into elite environments helping people become better human beings and better performers. Sweat & Courage was born in the darkest season of my own life. Today it has become a framework used inside professional football, universities and leadership environments.

Cornell Men's Soccer · John Smith

“I Was Winning. But It Wasn't Real.”

A top-25 program, four MLS draft picks, an MLS MVP — and a head coach who, behind the curtain, was done.

“I was winning. But it wasn't real. I was ready to quit.”John Smith — Head Coach, Cornell Men's Soccer

The Situation

Success on the Surface. Exhaustion Underneath.

A program climbing into the top 25 of the nation. Four MLS draft picks. One MLS MVP now in the US National Team.

And a head coach who, behind the curtain, was done. Exhausted. Masked up. Overthinking everything.

“I remember telling Drewe: I might have to quit. I didn't think I could do it anymore.”John Smith

The Work

From the Course to the Team.

John completed our leadership course, and then Drewe worked with the team directly through the Sweat & Courage app.

The shift wasn't about tactics. It was about a coach reconnecting with why he started — and leading from truth rather than performance.

If you would like a transformation like this, let's build better human beings and better performers together.

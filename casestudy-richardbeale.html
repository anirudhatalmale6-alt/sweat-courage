Richard Beale · West Bromwich Albion

Two Words That Guided a Win.

‘Sweat’ and ‘Courage’ guided West Brom to just their second win of the season — in Richard Beale's first match in caretaker charge.

“Sweat and Courage was number one, I make no apologies for repeating that. Obviously that translates to working really hard, running really hard.”Richard Beale

The Match

Play Without Fear. Get on the Front Foot.

“They delivered what he asked in spades, from front to back. Albion were purposeful in possession and resolute when asked to defend, most notably in the final 20 minutes.

On top of that, we moved the ball quickly, which was good. We knew we'd need to do that at times if they were in a block, to open up spaces — we did a lot of work on that this week.

Play without fear, get on the front foot. That's what they did today, which was brilliant. The rest will take care of itself.”
